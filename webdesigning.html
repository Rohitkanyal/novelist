There are 2 types of Coversite normally -

Static Coversite Designing: If you just want to have a Coversite with details about your company and products / services then this is simple and good option. In static Coversite also you have two options one is template based Coversite and other is completely customized designed Coversite. Template based Coversite is ready to use design in which logo, content, links and images are changed, but the layout of Coversite is not changed. Such Coversite are fast to deliver and economical in cost. Second is completely customized Coversite, in this our Cover designers work on your Coversite right from the scratch and do completely unique design. Such Coversite cost is higher than template based and takes longer time to complete, but such Coversites look are totally as per clients requirement.

Dynamic Coversite Designing & Development: If you have more products / services to be displayed on Coversite or you want to manage the Cover pages by your own, then you are actually looking for a dynamic Coversite. When we say a dynamic Coversite then it has a customized design and a CMS program to manage the Cover pages. Such Coversite are also known as CMS Coversites, in which admin can manage the whole Coversite from admin panel.
